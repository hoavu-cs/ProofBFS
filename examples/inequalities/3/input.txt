Definition: Let $a, b, c$ be positive real numbers such that $a + b + c \le 2$.
###
Fact: AM-GM inequality: for positive reals $x, y$, $x + y \ge 2\sqrt{xy}$, equivalently $\sqrt{xy} \le \frac{x+y}{2}$.
Comment: useful inequality
###
Fact: Cauchy-Schwarz: $\left(\sum_i u_i v_i\right)^2 \le \left(\sum_i u_i^2\right)\left(\sum_i v_i^2\right)$.
Comment: useful inequality
###
